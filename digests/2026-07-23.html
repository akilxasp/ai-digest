Daily Tip
If you're teaching an AI agent a repetitive task, don't just describe it — demonstrate it once, narrated, the way Anthropic's new Claude Cowork feature expects. A recorded walkthrough captures the edge cases and exact click-order that a written prompt usually leaves out, and it turns into a Skill you can rerun instead of re-explaining.
